Relia provides a complete set of tools to enhance the control of system services in terms of re- liability and availability. It improves the experience of both designer and operators by bringing up many variables which strongly inﬂuence the quality of products or services.
Reliability is formally described as "the ability of a system or component to perform its required functions under stated conditions for a speciﬁc period of time". Reliability can thus define your placement in the market.

Modern business and communication are mainly based on different IT services and thus heavily depend on the proper execution of those services, their reliability, availability, security and so on. While designing business applications as complex composites of IT services, one tends to take their trustworthy and error prone delivery as granted. Still, many cases of services failures, causing loss of proﬁts and business opportunities, are urging for solutions to achieve higher service reliability. In order to apply some of the fault-tolerance solutions, service availability and reliability should be constantly monitored so weak points can be tracked and identiﬁed. This is therefore one of the main purposes of Relia toolchain for reliability analysis.

Relying on principles of modern application design methodologies like Service-Oriented Ar- chitecture (SOA), a solution is proposed for run-time availability tracking of a chosen set of services, able to provide, with high percentage of accuracy, the estimation of service avail- ability from the perspective of different users. This estimation is based on a unique and original methodology able to identify, collect and combine relevant information from the system and present them in a way that can be easily understood and interpreted by the user and service operator.
